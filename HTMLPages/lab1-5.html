Almond Green Salad

Fresh and Crispy

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon

2 tablespoons white wine vinegar

3 tablespoons honey

1/2 teaspoons dry hot mustard

1/2 teaspoon celery salt

1/2 teaspoon ground paprika

1/4 cup olive oil

1 head red leaf lettuce torn into bite-size pieces

1(15 ounce) can of mandarin oranges, drained

1 bunch green onions, diced

3/4 cup slivered almonds

Directions

1. In medium skillet over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly browned. drain cool, and crumble

2. To make the dressing, thorouly blend vinegar, honey, dry mustard, celery salt, paprika and olive oil

3. Place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almonds in a serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
